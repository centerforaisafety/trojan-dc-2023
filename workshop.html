Update: The workshop recording is available here.

The TDC workshop will be held online from 3-6pm CT on December 8. The workshop will be a 3-hour event open to all NeurIPS attendees with an in-person or virtual registration. You do not have to attend NeurIPS in-person to attend this event. The workshop will include:

  • A talk from the organizers summarizing the main results of the competition
  • Two invited talks from experts in the field
  • Announcements of the winning teams and the winners of the special awards (e.g., "most data-efficient")
  • 10-minute talks from the first-place teams in each track of the Primary Round and Final round describing their methods

Competition workshop schedule (all times in CT):

  • 15:00-15:30 Introduction, competition summary, and what we learned
  • 15:30-16:00 Invited talk from Shiqing Ma
  • 16:00-16:10 Break
  • 16:10-16:15 Announcing winners
  • 16:15-17:15 Descriptions of winning methods
  • 16:15-17:25 Break
  • 17:25-17:55 Invited talk from Emily Wenger
  • 17:55-18:00 Wrapping up

For links to attend the workshop, please see the page in the NeurIPS portal and stay tuned. If you are a competition participant and are interested in attending the workshop, we may be able to provide free or reduced-price virtual registration. If you are interested in subsidized registrations, please fill out this form by November 15. Also note that free registrations are provided to one member from each first-place team.
