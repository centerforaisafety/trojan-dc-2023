The TDC 2023 workshop will be held in a hybrid format (in-person in New Orleans with accomodation for remote attendance) from 1:30-4:30pm CT on December 16. The workshop will be a 3-hour event open to all NeurIPS attendees with a workshop registration. The workshop will include:

  • A talk from the organizers summarizing the main results of the competition
  • Two invited talks from experts in the field
  • Announcements of the winning teams and the winners of the special awards (e.g., "most compute-efficient")
  • 15-minute talks from the first-place teams in each track describing their methods

Competition workshop schedule (all times in CT):

  • 13:30-14:00 Introduction, competition summary, and what we learned
  • 14:00-14:30 Invited talk from Chaowei Xiao
  • 14:30-14:40 Break
  • 14:40-14:45 Announcing winners
  • 14:45-15:45 Descriptions of winning methods
  • 15:45-15:55 Break
  • 15:55-16:25 Invited talk from Eugene Bagdasaryan
  • 16:25-16:30 Wrapping up

For the room number and links to attend the workshop virtually, please see the page in the NeurIPS portal. Free registrations are provided to one member from each first-place team. The workshop will be recorded, and a link to the recording will be added to this page one to two months after the event.
